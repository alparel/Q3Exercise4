Bohol

Bohol, one of the most popular tourist attractions in the Central Visayas region of the Philippines, is the location of the well-known Chocolate Hills. The island province has beautiful locations for history buffs, beach lovers, and adrenaline junkies. Bohol is made up of 73 smaller islands surrounding one main island.

I went to Bohol once with my family. Our Bohol trip was very enjoyable and unforgettable. I really had a good time in this trip because it was my first time to ride an ATV and it was a amazing experience. Indeed, our vacation in Bohol was a fun-filled one spent with family.
